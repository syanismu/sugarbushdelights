Associations

Cakes by Beatriz

Gisella did an apprenticeship under Beatriz Muller for many years before opening up her own business during Covid. Beatriz is an award-winning Master Sugar Artist and owner of Cakes By Beatriz and Gingerneers who has appeared on television multiple times. Working with one of Canada's greatest Sugar Artist, Gisella has become an artist of her own and an official member of the Gingerneers. The Gingerneers are designers of unique and custom gingerbread pieces from cookies to complex buildings. Working with Beatriz has allowed Gisella to be on the Food Network on two seperate occasions as well as work on projects for corporations such as William's Sonoma in NYC and MLSE in Toronto.
